WORKPLACE CONDUCT POLICY

Section 1: Professional Behavior
Employees are expected to maintain professional conduct at all times. This includes:
- Treating all colleagues with respect and courtesy
- Communicating clearly and professionally
- Avoiding personal conflicts in the workplace
- Maintaining a clean and organized work environment
- Being punctual and meeting deadlines

Section 2: Attendance and Time Management
Regular attendance is essential for organizational success. Employees should:
- Arrive on time for their scheduled shifts
- Notify management immediately of any absences
- Request time off in advance when possible
- Account for all work hours accurately
- Participate in team meetings and events

Section 3: Communication Standards
Effective communication is crucial for workplace harmony. Employees must:
- Use professional language in all communications
- Respond to emails and messages promptly
- Participate constructively in meetings
- Report issues through proper channels
- Maintain confidentiality of sensitive information

Section 4: Code of Conduct
All employees must adhere to ethical standards:
- Avoid conflicts of interest
- Do not accept inappropriate gifts or favors
- Report unethical behavior
- Comply with all company policies
- Support diversity and inclusion initiatives

Section 5: Compliance and Safety
Workplace safety is everyone's responsibility:
- Follow all safety protocols and procedures
- Report hazards immediately
- Use protective equipment as required
- Participate in safety training
- Maintain a substance-free workplace

Section 6: Confidentiality and Data Security
Protecting company information is critical:
- Keep trade secrets confidential
- Do not share proprietary information
- Use secure passwords and two-factor authentication
- Report data breaches immediately
- Follow data protection regulations

Section 7: Performance and Accountability
Employees are accountable for their performance:
- Set and meet performance goals
- Seek feedback from supervisors
- Participate in performance reviews
- Take responsibility for mistakes
- Continuously improve skills and knowledge

Section 8: Consequences of Misconduct
Violations of this policy may result in:
- Verbal warnings
- Written warnings
- Suspension
- Termination of employment
- Legal action in severe cases

Section 9: Questions and Support
For questions about workplace conduct:
- Contact your direct supervisor
- Reach out to Human Resources
- Use the anonymous reporting hotline
- Review the full employee handbook
- Attend training sessions

Last Updated: May 2026
Effective for all current and future employees
